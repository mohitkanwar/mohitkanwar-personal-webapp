My Public Information

This project aims at identifying different public information of the user. As a non-technical user we do not realize how much information we release into public without realizing. As a developer and explorer, I would like to collate all the ways of getting that information and sharing the ways of identifying and tracking it. I will start from easy to track things to more difficult items.
